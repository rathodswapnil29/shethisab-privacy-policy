Privacy Policy – ShetHisab (शेतीचा हिशोब)

Effective Date: 27 June 2026

1. Data Collection

ShetHisab does not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number. All farming records including crops, expenses, income, and loans are stored locally on your device.

2. Weather Data

The app uses third-party weather APIs to provide weather updates and farming advisory. Location may be used only for weather forecasts.

3. Market Price Data

Mandi (Bazaar Bhav) data is fetched from public agricultural market sources like MSAMB or Agmarknet.

4. Backup & Restore

Users can create local backups on their device. Backup files are not uploaded to our servers.

5. Data Deletion

Users can permanently delete all app data using the Reset option inside the app.

6. Security

All data remains on the user’s device. We do not sell, share, or transfer user data.

7. Third-party Services

The app may use weather APIs and analytics tools to improve functionality.
